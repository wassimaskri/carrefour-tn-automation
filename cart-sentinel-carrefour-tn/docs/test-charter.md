# Exploratory Test Charter

## Charter

Explore Carrefour Tunisia as a first-time customer who wants to find a product, understand its price, add it to the basket and recover if the product is unavailable or the journey is interrupted.

## Personas

| Persona | Goal | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Busy shopper | Find water quickly and add it to cart | Search or cart friction |
| Returning customer | Access account and saved products | Broken login/wishlist routing |
| Offline-first customer | Find nearest store | Store locator unavailable |
| Support-seeking customer | Reach FAQ/contact links | Help path hidden or broken |

## Heuristics Used

- Conversion path: search, product, cart.
- Discoverability: header, menu, category entry points.
- Trust: price, footer links, social presence.
- Negative states: no-result search and empty newsletter submission.
- Session realism: anonymous user behavior is valid and expected.

## What I Would Ask Product/Business

- Which categories/products are considered critical for daily monitoring?
- Is cart add expected to work before selecting delivery mode?
- Should wishlist force authentication or allow guest save?
- Which support links are legally/commercially mandatory?
- What is the acceptable page load time for home, search and PDP?
